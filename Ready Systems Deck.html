01 — Why now

The whole shop runs on registers and Excel

What that costs you

Slow — quoting lives in one person's head. Fragile — one file, lost rows, no backup. Invisible — no live view of stock or job status. Hard to audit — paper quality records.

How we fix it without disruption

We don't change how you build — you're the experts at that. We make the paperwork digital, searchable, backed-up and connected: one record per work order, moving through known stages, with the owner's costing know-how captured in software.

The principle

Digitize the factory first — then layer intelligence on top. Analytics and smart costing only work once the data exists to be intelligent about. Tally stays for accounting; we connect to it, we don't rebuild it.
